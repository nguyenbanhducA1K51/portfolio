    SIIM-ACR segmentation

    Pneumothorax can be caused by a blunt chest injury, damage from underlying lung disease, or most horrifying—it may occur for no obvious reason at all. This project attempted identify and segment Pneumothorax disease in chest x-rays using variations of U-Net architectures (UNet, ResUnet,DenseUnet). It has achieved the highest average dice score of 80% with U-Net employing ResNet34 as the encoder.

    Python, Pytorch, Numpy
